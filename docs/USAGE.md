# Usage Guide

## Basic Operations

### Adding a Receipt

1. Tap the **+** button on the home screen
2. Camera opens automatically
3. Take a photo of your receipt
4. Fill in the details:
   - Store name (required)
   - Amount (required)
   - Date (defaults to today)
   - Category (select from list)
   - Notes (optional)
5. Tap **Save Receipt**

### Viewing Receipts

- **List View**: Shows all receipts sorted by date (newest first)
- **Search**: Use the search bar to find receipts by store name, category, or notes
- **Detail View**: Tap any receipt to see full details and image

### Deleting a Receipt

1. Open the receipt detail page
2. Tap the trash icon in the top-right
3. Confirm deletion

## Data Storage

- **Database**: Hive (local NoSQL)
- **Images**: Local filesystem
- **Location**: App-specific directory
- **Backup**: Uninstalling removes all data

## Privacy

- All data stored locally on your device
- No internet connection required
- No data sent to external servers
- You have complete control over your data
